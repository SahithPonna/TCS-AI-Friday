Title: Common SEC Filings Overview
Jurisdiction: United States (SEC)
Hierarchy: SEC > Public Company Disclosure > Filings

Description:
This file lists the most common SEC filing types used by public companies and other market participants.
It is a simplified overview for training and demonstration purposes, not an exhaustive or official list.

Core Periodic and Registration Filings:
- Form S-1: Registration statement for newly issued securities (IPO or other offerings).
- Form S-3: Short-form registration statement for certain seasoned issuers.
- Form S-4: Registration statement for securities issued in business combinations (mergers, exchanges).
- Form 10-K: Annual report containing audited financial statements and detailed business information.
- Form 10-Q: Quarterly report with unaudited financial statements and MD&A.
- Form 8-K: Current report used to disclose material events between periodic filings.
- Form 20-F: Annual report for foreign private issuers.
- Form 40-F: Annual report for certain Canadian issuers under MJDS.
- Form F-1: Registration statement for foreign private issuers.

Ownership and Insider Reporting:
- Form 3: Initial statement of beneficial ownership of securities by insiders.
- Form 4: Statement of changes in beneficial ownership (insider trading/activity report).
- Form 5: Annual statement of beneficial ownership for certain transactions not reported on Form 4.
- Schedule 13D: Beneficial ownership report when acquiring >5% of a class of equity with control intent.
- Schedule 13G: Short-form beneficial ownership report (>5%) without intent to influence control.

Investment Company and Adviser Filings:
- Form N-1A: Registration for open-end mutual funds.
- Form N-2: Registration for closed-end funds.
- Form N-CEN: Annual census reporting for registered funds.
- Form N-PORT: Monthly portfolio holdings reporting for registered funds.
- Form ADV: Investment adviser registration and disclosure document.

Market Structure and Broker-Dealer Related Filings:
- Form ATS: Registration / notice for Alternative Trading Systems.
- Form ATS-N: Public filing for NMS stock ATSs.
- Form BD: Broker-dealer registration form.
- Form CAT Reports: Consolidated Audit Trail reporting by market participants (not a single form, but a reporting regime).
- Regulation NMS related submissions: Various filings to support market structure rules (e.g., rule changes, policies).

Other Selected Filings:
- Schedule TO: Tender offer statement.
- Schedule 14A: Proxy statement for shareholder meetings.
- Schedule 14D-9: Solicitation / recommendation statement in response to a tender offer.
- Form D: Notice of exempt offerings of securities (Regulation D private placements).
- Form PF: Private fund reporting by certain advisers (under SEC / CFTC joint rulemaking).
